— Our Story —

A Culinary Journey
Born in Coimbatore

Nestled in the heart of Coimbatore, Kadhambam is more than just a restaurant — it's an experience woven with warmth, tradition, and the finest flavours from across India and beyond.

Our chefs bring together the soul of South Indian comfort cooking, the richness of Mughlai cuisine, the zing of Indo-Chinese dishes, all under one roof. Whether you're celebrating milestones, enjoying a family dinner, or simply indulging in great food — Kadhambam is your home away from home.
